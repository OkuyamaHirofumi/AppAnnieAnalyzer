説明

Ever wondered who unfollowed you on Twitter? Who of the people you're following is not following you back?

Find out users who unfollow you, discover those users who are not following you back, keep track of new followers, easily and conveniently follow or unfollow users, enjoy multiple accounts support, and more...

* PLEASE NOTE: This app currently does not support users with more than 40,000 followers or following.
