説明

FREE online dating service for Facebook users.

One problem with free dating services, is because there is no cost to join the network, it's easy for fakes, frauds, scammers, and spammers to join and ruin the free dating service for everyone. Facebook Dating is part of the large and free Dating DNA dating network service, but allows Facebook users to verify that they have a valid Facebook account and lets other users see how many friends they have on Facebook. Because fakes and frauds won't usually go to the trouble to set up a Facebook account with lots of friends, just knowing that others in the network are also verified Facebook users, gives the users a lot more confidence that the other person is for real.

IMPORTANT NOTE!!! THIS APP NEVER POSTS ANYTHING TO YOU OR YOUR FRIEND'S FACEBOOK PAGES...EVER. The app only uses Facebook Connect to verify your Facebook account, but never posts anything to your pages, so your privacy is always assured.

FEATURES:

• 100% FREE SERVICE. NO MONTHLY FEES.
• REAL PEOPLE WITH REAL PHOTOS - Community Monitoring quickly tosses pervs, fakes and frauds.
• NO ADVERTISING in the app.
• QUICKLY IDENTIFY other verified Facebook users.
• Free, world-class support via email 24/7.
• In-depth Compatibility Matching (goes way beyond silly winks and pokes).
• "Compatibility Threshold" - Only those who meet your standards can view your profile or send you messages.
• Device-based Geo Location verifies the city & country others message from, keeping out the fakes.
• Push Notifications for new messages.
• At-a-Glance Compatibility Scoring.
• At-a-Glance Dating Potential Indicator.
• "Match Radar" - GPS enabled for finding nearby Matches when mobile.
• Chat rooms.
• Use Worldwide - Locate Matches within a fraction of a mile/km right next door or across the globe.
• Any sex - straight, gay, lesbian or bisexual.
• Extensive Friends List management.
• Email with historical Message Logs keeps all your conversations together.
• Multi-touch Photo Viewing.
• Optional Myers-Briggs personality profile included.
• Also use from your PC or Mac at www.datingdna.com website.
• Integration with Social Networks like Facebook and MySpace.

NOTE: You must be 18 years or older to join this dating network.

Video tour: http://www.datingdna.com/iphoneinfo

Run by volunteers who just want to see "dating done right," the Dating DNA website and service are 100% free of cost and contain no advertising. The only thing Dating DNA offers that costs anything is this $.99 app and the optional Plus edition of the iPhone app for $4.99.

EMAIL US with any problems, comments or suggestions at support@datingdna.com. Your input is greatly appreciated! We respond to EVERY email we receive.

バージョン 7.11 (2011年10月31日)

★ REMEMBER! High ratings keep us motivated to keep adding features, as well as attracting more users and Matches for you. :)

★ NEW! Emails and profiles are now tagged with location and device information. This means you can now verify the location (City and Country) of where a user is ACTUALLY emailing you from, not just from where they "say" they are located in their profile. This helps thwart scammers who say, for example, they live in Los Angeles, CA, but are really emailing you from some other country. If a user is running this version or later, on every email there will be a new "From" indicator that tells you the actual location the device was located in when sending the email. If the device is not capable of sending location information (email was sent from the website or the device had Location Services turned off for the Dating DNA app), then it will use the user's IP address to best calculate where they are located. For example, if it says "From iPhone in: Los Angeles, CA USA" then you can be certain that the email was sent from an iPhone that was physically in the LA area when the email was sent. If it says "From IP in: Los Angeles, CA USA" then it means it was sent using an IP address routed via LA. If you want to verify for certain a user's location, tell them to turn on their Location Services for the DNA app and to email you from within the app. Additionally, this location information will be displayed in the bottom section of a user's profile, if it's available, so you can view this with Matches, before you even share emails with someone. If you spot a user who has a location listed in their profile which is drastically different from the verified location information, please flag their profile so we can investigate and remove them from the system if necessary.

★ NEW! A new Unfriend button has been added to the email message logs and the old Block button has been renamed to Report & Block.

バージョン 7.05 (2011年7月30日)

Remember...High ratings keep us motivated to keep adding features. :)

★★★ Now easier than ever to use with new Express Interest, Not Interested, and Unfriend buttons! ★★★

• On the Matches profile pages, instead of a Delete button, we now have a "Not Interested" button. If you know you're not interested in someone, tapping on this button will remove both of you from each other's Matches, saving you both time by removing those who are not interested in each other from Matches.

• On the Matches profile pages, instead of an Add button, we now have an "Express Interest" button. Tapping on this button will notify the other person that you are interested in them. It will also add you both to each other's Friends Lists. This is the easiest way to quickly let those in your Matches know you're interested in them, without having to send a message. You can, of course, still send them a personalized message as well, if you wish.

• On the Friends profile page, instead of a Delete button, there is now an "Unfriend" button. When you Unfriend someone, they are removed from your Friends List, and you are now also removed from their Friend List as well. All messages between the two of you will also be removed. This makes it quick and easy to remove those who express interest in you, but you're not interested in return. Just Unfriend them.

• The lock/unlock feature for Friends has been removed, since this is now done automatically when you remove someone from your Friends List with the new "Unfriend" button.

• You can now add someone to your Friends List, even if you don't meet their Compatibility Threshold. The Compatibility Threshold is now just used to show or hide you in someone's Matches, but does not server to prevent someone from adding you to their Friends List or sending you a message.

• We removed the Thumbnail view option for photos and now only have the full-screen view.
