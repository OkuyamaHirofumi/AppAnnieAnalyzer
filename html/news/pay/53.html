説明

Fiery Feeds is a feed reader built for power users. It syncs with Feedly, Feedbin, Minimal Reader, Feed Wrangler, Fever, Feedja, NewsBlur, AOL Reader, Inoreader and The Old Reader.

- Background Updates -
Fiery can download updates to your feeds in the background so you have them when you need them.

- Sharing -
Fiery supports all the usual suspects out of the box. Plus a ton of apps like Chrome, Tweetbot or OmniFocus. Fiery also supports custom URL schemes and customisable email templates.

- Keyboard Shortcuts -
You can use Fiery Feeds with a keyboard, without even touching the screen.

- VoiceOver -
Every feature is accessible via VoiceOver.

- Multiple Accounts -
You can add as many accounts to Fiery as you like.

- Color Themes -
Wherever you want a dark mode or more contrast, Fiery has got you covered. You can even install custom color themes.

- Fullscreen Reading -
Enjoy long form text in a distraction-less reading experience.

- Universal App -
Buy it once and use it on your iPhone and iPad.

- Sharing Services -
● Mail
● Messages
● Facebook
● Twitter
● Delicious
● Pinboard
● Instapaper
● Pocket
● Readability
● Buffer
● OneNote
● Evernote
● SendToReader
